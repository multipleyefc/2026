Abstract submission system and instructions for authors

Submission options are:
- Single oral presentation
- Poster presentation
- Symposium
The submission link is here.

Abstracts for oral presentations or posters should be limited to 1 page and should contain: Title of the presentation; Author information (name, affiliation, contact info); Main body of the text (Background and aims, Methodology, Results, Discussion).
Please use this abstract template form:
MultiplEYE Final Conference Abstract template

Abstracts for symposia must be submitted as one single document (max. 5 pages) and should include: Title of the symposium; Information on the symposium chair(s) (name, affiliation, contact info); Synopsis of the symposium (describing the scientific relevance of the symposium and its aims); Abstracts for up to five oral presentations (prepared according to the format for regular single oral presentation abstracts), The name of a discussant (affiliation and contact info). Only one submission per symposium is permitted. Papers included in a symposium cannot be submitted separately as single oral presentations. Symposia applications will be evaluated in their entirety.
