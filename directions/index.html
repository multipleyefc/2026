The conference will take place at Faculty of Information Sciences and Computer Engineering at the technical campus of Ss. Cyril and Methodius University in Skopje.

  • Address: ul. Rudzer Boshkovikj 16, 1000 Skopje, Republic of North Macedonia
  • Google Maps pin: https://maps.app.goo.gl/cMsG7VYJrzRLDXeA9

The faculty is easily accessible by public transport (city buses) as well as by taxi. A taxi ride from Skopje International Airport typically takes around 25–30 minutes and costs €25. Alternatively, I can help organise an airport transfer for €20 through a company we collaborate with (this can be discussed and arranged at a later stage if needed). There is also a shuttle bus from the airport to the city centre, with a ticket price of €3.50. According to the information provided on the shuttle service’s official website, departures are relatively limited. In addition, reaching the venue from the city centre requires a further local bus connection. For these reasons, and taking into consideration that summers in Skopje can be really hot, this option may be less convenient than an organized transfer.
