We are delighted to announce the MultiplEYE Final Conference!

2026 is the final year of the MultiplEYE COST Action, The purpose of the MultiplEYE Final Conference is to bring together researchers from different fields involved in the Action (and beyond!) to present their work. The scope of the conference focuses on the various aspects of the eye-tracking-while-reading data, thus reflecting the variety of activities conducted within the MultiplEYE Action. Submission topics can include (but are not limited to):

  • eye-tracking data collection and analyses
  • eye-tracking for machine learning
  • reading comprehension
  • individual differences in reading
  • the contribution of cognitive skills to reading (comprehension)
