Call for Abstracts

for the

MultiplEYE Final Conference

Advancing multilingual eye-tracking-while-reading research

The MultiplEYE COST Action invites the research community to submit abstracts to its final international conference on eye-tracking-while-reading, bringing together work from psycholinguistics, computational linguistics, NLP, and eye-tracking methodology.

Topics of Interest

We invite abstracts on a wide range of topics related to eye-tracking and reading behavior. Submission topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Eye-tracking data collection and pipeline standardization
  • Eye-tracking for Machine Learning and NLP tasks
  • Reading comprehension assessment and modeling
  • Individual differences in reading behavior
  • The contribution of cognitive skills to reading (comprehension)
  • Cross-linguistic analysis of eye-movement patterns
  • Reproducibility and transparency in eye-tracking research
