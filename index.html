We are delighted to announce the MultiplEYE Final Conference!

2026 is the final year of the MultiplEYE COST Action . We invite researchers from all related fields to join us in Skopje for our concluding conference. This event will serve as a platform to present our collective work on multilingual eye-tracking-while-reading data and to discuss future directions in releavnt research domains, including psycholinguistics, computational linguistics, and data science. We welcome submissions from Action members as well as external researchers interested in the analysis, collection, and application of eye-tracking data.

Topics of Interest

We invite abstracts on a wide range of topics related to eye-tracking and reading behavior. Submission topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Eye-tracking data collection and pipeline standardization
  • Eye-tracking for Machine Learning and NLP tasks
  • Reading comprehension assessment and modeling
  • Individual differences in reading behavior
  • The contribution of cognitive skills to reading (comprehension)
  • Cross-linguistic analysis of eye-movement patterns
  • Reproducibility and transparency in eye-tracking research
