Fees and Catering

Conference Fee: €0 (Free)
Catering: Coffee breaks will be provided at the venue for all registered participants on both days of the conference.
Social Dinner: A conference dinner will be held on the evening of August 20th. Please indicate your attendance in the registration form. A separate payment will be required for the dinner.

Financial Support (COST Reimbursement)

Funding for travel and daily allowance is available through the COST Action CA21131. Please note that registering for the conference does not automatically guarantee reimbursement.
Eligibility for Reimbursement: Priority will be given to Action members with an accepted abstract and early career researchers.
How to Apply: You can request funding when filling out the registration form. Formal invitations for reimbursement will be sent via e-COST after the notification of abstract acceptance (June 2026).

Visa Information

If you require a formal invitation letter for visa purposes, please indicate this in the registration form.

Privacy Notice
